# Tavern & Quest SaaS

> *"From dungeon crawl to SaaS install"*

## Company Overview

**Tavern & Quest** is a B2B SaaS platform serving adventuring guilds across the Sword Coast. Founded in the basement of the Yawning Portal inn, T&Q helps guild masters manage quests, track loot distribution, and coordinate party composition.

### The Product

- **Quest Board** — Post, claim, and track adventures
- **Party Finder** — Match adventurers by class, level, and availability
- **Loot Ledger** — Fair treasure splitting with audit trails
- **Guild Analytics** — Track success rates, casualties, and gold-per-quest metrics

### Key Personas

| Persona | Role | Pain Points |
|---------|------|-------------|
| **Guildmaster Thrain** | Guild Admin | Can't track which quests are profitable; losing members to rival guilds |
| **Lyra Shadowstep** | Freelance Rogue | Hard to find parties that need her skills; payment disputes |
| **Sir Aldric** | Party Leader | Scheduling nightmares; no way to vet new party members |
